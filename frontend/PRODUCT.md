# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Neo ETF serves crypto-native investors and developers using Sepolia to understand, test, and operate an onchain index product. Their primary jobs are to inspect holdings, invest or redeem with a chosen token, stake NETF, claim NRWD rewards, and obtain test assets without ambiguity.

## Product Purpose

Neo ETF turns a multi-asset onchain basket into a legible, trustworthy workflow. Success means users understand exactly what asset they are using, what they will receive, what stage a transaction is in, and how the resulting balances changed.

## Brand Personality

Credible, restrained, technical. Calm enough for financial decisions, explicit enough for irreversible blockchain actions, and modern without using speculative Web3 visual clichés.

## Anti-references

- Neon cyberpunk Web3 dashboards.
- Decorative glassmorphism and purple gradient surfaces.
- Generic SaaS card mosaics.
- Interfaces that hide contract state, transaction progress, or failure causes.
- Dense terminal aesthetics that make ordinary investors decode the product.

## Design Principles

1. Show chain truth, never placeholder finance data.
2. Make irreversible actions explain themselves before wallet confirmation.
3. Use one calm visual vocabulary across every workflow.
4. Reveal complexity progressively: essential outcome first, technical evidence second.
5. Preserve user orientation through loading, approval, confirmation, success, and failure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast. Every action must be keyboard reachable with a visible focus state. Color never carries status alone. Touch targets are at least 44px. Motion respects reduced-motion preferences.
